Pile 'em up

Description

This dish from the popular game Genshin Impact is an explosion of flavors and colors. It features a mountain of various ingredients such as meat, vegetables, and spices, all layered skillfully to create an inviting and flavorful dish. The combination of tastes and textures makes the 'Pile 'Em Up' a delicious and satisfying meal for every adventurer venturing into the world of Teyvat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 300g of your choice of meat (chicken, pork, or beef), diced
  • 2 carrots, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh ginger, grated
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh chopped scallions (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the rice according to package instructions and set it aside.
  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and ginger and saut√© for about a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the meat to the skillet and cook until it's browned on all sides.
  4. Add the vegetables (carrots, bell pepper, and onion) to the skillet and cook for another 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  5. Add the soy sauce to the skillet and stir well to ensure all ingredients are well coated.
  6. Taste and adjust salt and pepper if needed.
  7. To serve, distribute the rice onto individual plates and top with the meat and vegetable mixture.
  8. Garnish with fresh chopped scallions if desired.
  9. Enjoy your homemade "Pile 'Em Up"!
